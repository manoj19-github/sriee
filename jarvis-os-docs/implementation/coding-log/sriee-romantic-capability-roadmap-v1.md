## Safety and product behavior

Romantic content is opt-in and cannot affect permissions. It stops after rejection and is suppressed during approvals, emergencies, distress, financial/security decisions and age-uncertain contexts. Generated entertainment cannot fabricate shared real-world memories, imitate a real partner without consent, or reproduce long copyrighted passages.
